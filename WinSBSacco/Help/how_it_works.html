Soft Books Sacco - How it Works

Home

  1. Soft Books Sacco is a system developed to meet the entire needs of a Sacco organisation in a growing environment.
  2. The system comprises of various operational and management modules that will greatly assist in the day-to-day management of a Sacco. The modules are integrated into an overall system and they function inter-dependently to provide a collective Sacco resource planning and management tool.
  3. The system is also fully integrated with Acrobat Reader to produce all its reports in PDF format thus ensuring easy portability of reports. E.g. the reports can be saved on a disk and printed elsewhere with no difficulty at all.
  4. All transactional reports and financial statements can easily be produced without any additional manual work and can even be printed immediately after a transaction.
  5. The system offers a flexible chart of accounts where users can define their own general ledger codes and accounts.
  6. A system designed to manage needs of saving and credit cooperative organizations (sacco), matatu saccos,welfare groups, chamas and other micro-finance organizations; promoting accountability, transparency, enhancing efficiency and cost cuttting.
